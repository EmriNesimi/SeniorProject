Dangerous links may lead to phishing websites, download malware, or steal personal information. Always check the URL source and avoid clicking unknown shortened links.

What Types of Files Are Suspicious?

Executable files (.exe, .bat), unknown scripts, or even PDFs with embedded code can contain malware. Avoid downloading from untrusted sources and scan all files before opening.
